Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Score Tone handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

Google API Services & User Data

Score Tone integrates with Google API Services to allow users to open PDF files from their Google Drive. We strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Score Tone (Google Drive & Picker Integration)

Data Access & Collection: Score Tone requests OAuth permission to access sheet music files stored in your Google Drive using the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file and https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email scopes. The Drive scope is used to display the Google Picker, allowing you to browse and select which scores to open. The email scope is used to retrieve your email address, which is saved locally as a login hint to streamline future silent authentication requests. We only access the specific metadata and content of files you select.

Data Usage: We use your Google user data solely to download and render the PDF music scores in your local browser session as requested. We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any other business purpose.

Data Sharing & Transfers: We do not share, transfer, or disclose any Google user data or file content with third parties. All access and processing are handled entirely client-side in your browser.

Data Retention & Deletion: We do not store or retain your Google Drive files or metadata on any external servers. If you choose to cache a file for offline access, the PDF file content and its metadata are stored securely on your local device within the browser's IndexedDB storage. You can delete these cached files and their records at any time by clicking the trash icon in your library.

Login Hint & Email Storage: To streamline future authentication requests and support silent token refreshes, Score Tone retrieves your Google account email address via Google's user information endpoint and saves it locally on your device. This email is stored solely in your browser's local storage (localStorage) as a login hint, is never sent to Score Tone's servers, and is not shared with any third parties.

Session Security & OAuth Tokens: To maintain your connection across page refreshes, your temporary Google access token is stored in your browser's temporary session storage (sessionStorage). This token is automatically deleted when you close the browser tab or when you click the "Disconnect" button.

Security & Protection: Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. All communication between the application, your browser, and Google APIs is fully encrypted in transit using industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) encryption.

General Data Handling

  • No Account Required (Qualified): You do not need to create an account with us to use our tools. Local PDF files and settings require no registration. Connecting a Google account is optional and is only used to download and display PDF scores from your Google Drive.
  • Local Storage & IndexedDB: Any application configurations, preferences, or score metadata are stored locally in your browser (via LocalStorage or IndexedDB) and remain entirely on your device.
  • No Analytics/Tracking: We do not track your usage habits or sell data to advertising networks.

Third-Party Terms & Revocation

By using Score Tone, you also agree to be bound by Google's main Privacy Policy.

You can revoke access to your Google account at any time. To manage your app permissions, visit the Google Security Settings Page.
