Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Score Tone handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience.

Google API Services & User Data

Score Tone integrates with Google API Services to allow users to open PDF files from their Google Drive. We strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Score Tone (Google Drive & Picker Integration)

Data Access & Collection: Score Tone requests OAuth permission to view sheet music files stored in your Google Drive (drive.readonly or drive.file scopes) via the Google Picker. We only access the specific metadata and content of the PDF files you select.

Data Usage: We use your Google user data solely to download and render the PDF music scores in your local browser session as requested. We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or any other business purpose.

Data Sharing & Transfers: We do not share, transfer, or disclose any Google user data or file content with third parties. All access and processing are handled entirely client-side.

Data Retention & Deletion: We do not store or retain your Google Drive files or metadata on any external servers. File data is held temporarily in the browser's memory and is discarded immediately when you close the file view or browser tab.

Security & Protection: Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. All communication between the application, your browser, and Google APIs is fully encrypted in transit using industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) encryption.

General Data Handling

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account with us to use our tools.
  • Local Storage: Any application configurations, preferences, or score metadata are stored locally in your browser (via LocalStorage or IndexedDB) and remain entirely on your device.
  • No Analytics/Tracking: We do not track your usage habits or sell data to advertising networks.

Third-Party Terms & Revocation

By using Score Tone, you also agree to be bound by Google's main Privacy Policy.

You can revoke access to your Google account at any time. To manage your app permissions, visit the Google Security Settings Page.
